Hi there! Call me Marqui. I'm an IT graduate with almost 2 years of work experience. I've always loved technology and whatever involved in it such as Digital Design, Web design- well mainly, design. I've always been fascinated with any ideas involving it, vector illustration, color, images, layouts, photography, infographics, video editing, UI/UX and all that. I'm definitely starting the portfolio for the development of this particular interest and skill.

My most recent job was in Accenture Philippines as an Associate Software Engineer. I worked in the information technology and application support industry. Skilled in Adobe Design Programs, Visual Basic, Microsoft Office, Digital Photography, HTML5, CSS3 and basic knowledge in JavaScript.

As for graphic designing, I was an intern and my experience involved logo designing, infographics, and infomercial for a specific department. I was also a short-time freelance logo maker back in The Philippines. I am currently located in Dubai, UAE.

I am looking for an opportunity to maximize my skills for creative design in the technology industry.

I discovered my insatiable curiousity for design when I began doodling random names on any whitespace I could find back in highschool. It kind of upgraded when I started college as it brought me a fuelled sense of satisfaction when creativity is involved. I can solve problems through it and everything feels light and right. Inevitably, this led me onto a path of self-discovery.
