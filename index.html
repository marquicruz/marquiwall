Introducing Me.

I'm currently working as a Web UI/UX designer, managing SEO and partly doing graphic design for a technology consultancy firm in Dubai, UAE. I have worked in Accenture Philippines as an Associate Software Engineer working in the information technology and application support industry.
Skilled in Adobe Design Programs, Visual Basic, Microsoft Office, SQL, SEO, Digital Photography,Wordpress, HTML5, CSS3, SASS with basic knowledge in JavaScript.

As for graphic designing, I was an intern and my experience involved logo designing, infographics, and infomercial for a specific department. I was also a short-time freelance logo maker back in The Philippines. I am currently located in Dubai, UAE.

I am always looking for an opportunity to maximize my skills for creative design in the technology industry.

I discovered my insatiable curiousity for design when I began doodling random names on any whitespace I could find back in elementary. It kind of upgraded as I continue in highschool and eventually in my college days as it brought me a fuelled sense of satisfaction when creativity is involved. I can solve problems through it and everything feels light and right. Inevitably, this led me onto a path of self-discovery.
