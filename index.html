Hello, welcome to my humble little world.

Currently working in the UAE as a designer including handling of branding requirements, CRM assistance, CMS maintenance, photography/video editting and content creation. I have worked for a technology company that specializes in providing and developing an Internet of Things (IoT) platform in Dubai, UAE. I was focused mainly doing UI/UX, SEO, Website creation and Digital/Print design for it.
