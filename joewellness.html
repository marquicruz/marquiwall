JOE-WELLNESS CENTER

Joewellness logo has been created for a wellness center business of one of my relatives. We came up with an idea of combining a bit of a red and more of a green color that most likely describe wellness and health.

Sans Serif with a light text has been chosen for the font to conclude an elegant outcome.
